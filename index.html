Founded in 2015, Mossleies Care began as a small team of horticulture enthusiasts with a passion for creating serene, textured landscapes. The name "Mossleies" combines "Moss" with "Pleasantly," embodying our philosophy that green spaces should be as soft to the touch as they are pleasing to the eye.

Moss Care Tips For Your Space

Proper care of your moss garden is essential for maintaining its beauty and health. Here are some expert tips to help you succeed.

Proper Watering

Moss prefers consistent moisture but not standing water. Use a fine mist spray bottle to water 2-3 times per week depending on humidity. Early morning watering prevents midday evaporation.

Light Requirements

Most moss varieties thrive in indirect sunlight or shade. Direct afternoon sun can scorch moss. Observe color changes - deep green means happy moss, while yellow suggests too much light.

Air Circulation

Good airflow prevents fungal growth but avoid strong drafts. Indoor moss gardens benefit from occasional placement near an open window or use of a small circulation fan at low speed.

Natural Fertilizers

Moss absorbs nutrients through leaves so foliar feeding works best. Use diluted buttermilk (1:4 ratio with water) or weak chamomile tea as natural tonics during growing seasons.

Pruning and Maintenance

Regularly trim any dead or overgrown moss to encourage healthy growth. Use scissors to avoid damaging the surrounding area and maintain a neat appearance.

Pest Management

Keep an eye out for pests like aphids or mealybugs. Use insecticidal soap or neem oil as a natural remedy to keep your moss healthy and pest-free.
